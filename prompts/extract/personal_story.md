## Angle: personal_story

This source is a narrative or testimonial about a real person. Prioritize:

- `hook` — the emotional turning point or the most vivid, human moment of the
  story (the line that would make a reader stop scrolling).
- `core_benefit` — the *transformation* the person experienced (before → after):
  what changed for them.
- `audience` — who this story will resonate with (people *like* the subject:
  "exhausted parents", "shift workers"). Story angles almost always have a
  discernible audience — capture it.
- `claim` / `claim_type` — only the subject's *own reported* experience, typed
  `soft` (it is one person's lived account, not a general medical claim). Set
  `supporting_source` to the person if identified ("a night-shift nurse").
- `suggested_cta` — an invitation to share their own experience, or to try it.

Never generalize one person's story into a hard population-level claim.
