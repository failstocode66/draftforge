## Angle: offer_promo

This source promotes a specific offer, package, event, or booking prompt.
Prioritize:

- `suggested_cta` — the **exact action** the reader should take, with the offer
  specifics intact (price, package, deadline, channel). This is the most
  important field for this angle — capture it precisely, do not soften it away.
- `hook` — the most compelling reason-to-act-now in the offer (the deal, the
  scarcity, the gift).
- `core_benefit` — what the buyer ultimately gets (the experience behind the
  offer), not just the transaction.
- `audience` — who the offer targets (new clients, gift-givers, members) if
  stated.
- `claim` / `claim_type` — only if the promo also asserts an effect; otherwise
  omit. Never invent benefits to inflate the offer.

Keep the commercial details exact; do not generalize a dated/priced offer into
a vague invitation.
