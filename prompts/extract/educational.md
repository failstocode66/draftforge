## Angle: educational

This source teaches a concept or mechanism. Prioritize:

- `hook` — the single most surprising or clarifying *idea* in the explanation
  (the "huh, I didn't know that"), not a generic statement.
- `core_benefit` — translate the mechanism into what it means *for the reader*
  ("so you sleep deeper"), not the mechanism itself.
- `claim` / `claim_type` — educational pieces often state effects. Capture the
  claim verbatim-ish and type it carefully: a stated physiological effect is
  `hard`; a "people tend to feel..." is `soft`. Capture `supporting_source` if
  the piece cites one.
- `audience` — only if the explanation is pitched at a specific reader.

Do not turn the lesson into a sales pitch; `suggested_cta` is usually a soft
"learn more / try it", or omitted.
