## Angle: benefit_spotlight

This source centers on one concrete outcome of floating. Prioritize:

- `core_benefit` — the **single** headline benefit, stated crisply and
  specifically (e.g. "fall asleep faster for days afterward", not "wellness").
  Resist listing several; pick the dominant one.
- `hook` — a punchy framing of that benefit that could open a post.
- `audience` — who most wants this specific benefit (athletes for recovery,
  insomniacs for sleep). Capture when the benefit implies a clear reader.
- `claim` / `claim_type` — if the benefit is stated as an effect, capture and
  type it (be conservative: prefer `soft` unless an objective physiological
  effect is explicitly asserted). Capture `supporting_source` if named.
- `suggested_cta` — a direct nudge to experience the benefit ("book a float").

One benefit, sharply drawn — depth over breadth.
